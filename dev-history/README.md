# Provenance

The full paper trail behind this study — the preregistration (v2 and v3 plus adjudicated
amendments), the decision log, the freeze checklist and seal, the agent dispatch packets, and
the independent methodologist's rulings — **exists and was preserved**, but is intentionally
**not included in this public repository**.

Two reasons:

1. **Personal / operational detail.** Those documents name people, reference other private
   projects, and carry machine-specific paths, session identifiers, and operational notes
   that don't belong in a public repository.
2. **Benchmark integrity.** The task battery's answer keys (reference solutions and planted
   "canaries") are withheld so the benchmark isn't spoiled for anyone who might take it.

What the provenance trail demonstrates is that the
study was **preregistered before data collection**, that every design decision was logged,
and that the apparatus was **frozen and sealed** before runs began. The most important entry
in that trail (the decision to halt grading rather than produce a result around a broken
apparatus) is summarized in [`../RESULTS.md`](../RESULTS.md).

If you're evaluating this work and want to see the full preregistration or decision log,
ask — they can be shared privately.
